Gender Identities Pronouns Sexual Orientations More Info

Neopronouns are the terms that a person is comfortable using that are not the common pronouns that most people are aware of. If you use neopronouns, you will have to tell those that you speak to or change your display name to include your set of pronouns. Common neopronouns include ne/nim, xe/xir, ze/hir, and per/pers. Given that Max uses xe/xir pronouns, instead of saying "Max went to the store," someone could say "xe went to the store."
