Transgender refers to a person who identifies with a gender that is not the same as the gender they were assigned at birth. Some change their appearance, mannerisms, and behavior to fit their true gender identity, even taking hormones and having surgery to fully transition. These are optional as your gender identity is enough, but it can be a person's own preference to fully transition.
