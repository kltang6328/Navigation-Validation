Gender Identities Pronouns Sexual Orientations More Info

Polysexuality is the attraction to many genders. Similar to pansexual in that they are attracted to multiple genders, but polysexuals are not necessarily attracted to all genders. Also similar to bisexuality and are sometimes used interchangeably, but others highlight the significance of having both terms as polysexuals try to extend from the "binary" genders of male and female that bisexuality implies.
