Gynesexual refers to being sexually attracted to women or feminine features. Their partner doesn't necessarily have to be female or have female anatomy, but presenting or behaving in a feminine way is what attracts gynesexuals. This differs from homosexuality and heterosexuality in that the individual's own gender isn't in relation to their sexuality.
