Gender Identities Pronouns Sexual Orientations More Info

Sexual orientations signify who a person is comfortable getting into a sexual relationship with. These are different from romantic orientations in that someone may be comfortable dating certain genders, but not comfortable engaging into sexual activities with them. Also these are the better known sexualities, but there are many more, so if you feel that these do not accurately represent how you feel about yourself, take time to reserach more at the links in the tab labeled More Info.
