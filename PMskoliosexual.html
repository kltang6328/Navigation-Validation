Skoliosexuals are attracted to people who are not the gender they were assigned at birth, such as nonbinary or transgender people. The preferred term is ceterosexual as skolio/scolio refers to being bent or imperfect. The concept of ceterosexuality is still controversial as many believe the fact that a person is not cisgender shouldn't be attractive, but rather the potential partner's gender identity should be a bigger factor.
